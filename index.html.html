Mirror gap

The asymmetry between what Switzerland reports exporting to each GCC state and what that state reports importing from Switzerland.

Reading the gap. A negative gap means the GCC state records more imports than Switzerland records exports — consistent with re-routing through entrepôt hubs, CIF/FOB valuation differences, or timing effects. A positive gap means Swiss-reported exports exceed partner-reported imports. Persistent, structured gaps are an analytical signal, not a data error.
